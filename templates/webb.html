Casa Lakshmi is really all about the view. Located meters away from stunning serenity beach, the USP is really the view. From the pictures it appears to be a much larger space- but is smaller than what we expected. The ground floor has a small sitting space, a kitchen and a bathroom and the first floor has a bedroom and a stand-alone toilet. The entire place is cosy and the bedroom on the first floor has an incredible view. The place is great for a couple ( would not recommend with kids). Serenity beach is strategically located and provides easy access to both White town and central Pondy as well as to Auroville. It is a fisherman's beach and you will see lots of activity in the day- but we were never disturbed and it was in fact fascinating to see them at work. The beach becomes a fish market on Sunday and you will face some trouble getting your car out ( if you need to step out) as there are hundreds of bikes parked ( folks who come to buy fish) - all this clears up by around noon. So plan your sunday morning keeping this in mind ( we were not aware of how the beach gets transformed on Sundays). What's great: - view - location - ease-of check in (Kapoor was available at the house as promised and we had no issues checking-in or out. Our interaction with him was minimal- but pleasant) What can get better within the house itself: - Better blankets - Toiletries: liquid soaps in the bathrooms- we had to buy one for the toilet upstairs - Place needs a mosquito bat ( carry along a repellant) - overall better cleanliness In summary, good value for money- if you are a couple (or single) and happy with the space available.
