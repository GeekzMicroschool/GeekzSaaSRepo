Studios

School reimagined.

Kindergarten

Kindergarten Studio is to empower kids aged up to 4.5. Geekz integrates real life scenarios in KS curriculum & simulate it in the studio as Quests with ZERO Screen Time, to learn life-skills. Our trans-disciplinary learning includes US & Australian Phonics, English Language Arts, Math, Art & Craft, Dance & Quest (STEM).

Lower Elementary

LE Studio is part of Elementary Studio to empower kids aged 4.5 to 07. Geekz integrates real world careers in ES curriculum & simulate it in the studio as Quests, to get kids ready for the world in 2040. Our trans-disciplinary learning includes Language Arts English, Spanish, Elective Hindi/Regional, Math, Civilization (History & Geo), Art & Craft, Dance & Quest (STEM).

Upper Elementary

UE Studio is to empower kids aged 07 to 10. As it is part of Elementary Studio, integrates real world careers such as Astronaut, Embedded Engineer, Aviator, Doctor, Film Maker, Entrepreneur etc. in custom designed ES (CBSE, ICSE, IB, Finland & US Common Core) trans-disciplinary curriculum and simulate it in the studio as Quests (STEM). LES & UES operates together as Elementary Studio (ES).

Curriculum

Reimagined curriculum designed by Geekz Microschool.

Language Arts

Medium of Instruction is English. Irrespective of Mother Tongue, kids will learn to cummunicate in English.

Foreign Language

Learn foreign languages starting with Spanish in ES & many more of your choice from Middle School.

Elective Language

Learn Hindi/Regional Language of your choice as an Elective Language.

Math

Learn Math at your own pace in our studios. Same age kids, one learns Addition but the other Multiplication per their pace.

Civilization

History & Geography from 5000 BC till now, Nomads to Egyptian Civilization to Harappan to Mayan to Civil War to World Wars.

Art & Craft (Garage)

Learn art, painting styles, techniques & crafting with garage items, integrated with core skills (subjects) & quests.

Music & Dance

Performance arts, music, dance, sports & 21st century life skills offered part of curriculum.

Quest

50+ real world quests offered across 10+ years of schooling to help kids find their passion, get exposure & launch to the real world.
