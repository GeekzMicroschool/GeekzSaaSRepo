WHAT IS QUESTSCHOOL?

Afterschool reimagined.

QUESTSCHOOL

Questschool is an after school studio to empower kids aged 4.5 to 10 to find their passion & become a visionary, through hands-on Quests. Kids struggling in school curriculum will learn Science & Tech with flying colors through real world simulations.

QUEST

We integrate real world careers such as Astronaut, Embedded Engineer, Aviator, YouTuber, Film Maker, Entrepreneur etc. in CBSE, ICSE, IB, Finland & US Common Core Science, Math, Technology, Coding, Robotics & IOT curriculum and simulate it in the studio as Quests (STEM).

PASSION

Many kids hate to memorize science concepts. Instead of asking fish to climb the tree, Geekz find their passion through various hands-on Quests, integrate school curriculum in their passion & help them learn along with 21st century life skills by designing, building & experiencing it hands-on.

LAUNCHPAD

Instead of doing Engineering due to external influence & regretting in the late 20s, kids can try 50+ hands-on Quests across 10 years from the age 5 to 14, find their passionate career by the age of 14, pick their high school career stream accordingly & launch to the real world.

FAQ

Does Questschool Conduct In-Person Quest?

Yes. Few Quests are meant to be done at Questschool studio (home) as they're powerful with human touch & kids love it. We're starting with Online and post COVID, we will do physical In-Person Quests as well.


What Should I Do To Host In-Person Quests In Future & Online Quests Now?

While applying to Become a Questschool, choose "Both" online & in-person options for "School Mode Of Operation...". If you want to host only Online Quests ever, then go for "Online" option.


How Does A Geekz Questschool Gets Students?

Through digital ad campaigns run by Geekz team on social media platforms & you making your Questschool well known locally. In both ways, key is the free trial. Conduct free demo class to allure parents & students registered for free trial.


What Is The Duration & Max Student Headcount Per Quest?

Each Quest is 6 weeks, 2 classes a week & each class being 1 hour. Total 12 hours per Quest. Maximum 6 students per Quest with individually personalized as well as group team missions to simulate real life work environment.


How Much Does A Questschool Guide Earn?

On successful completion of the Quest, you will be paid Rs 700 per student per Quest which will be direct-deposited to your bank account at the EOM. In general you will earn more than an entry level software professional. More Quests, More Earnings!


What Are The Best Hours For Questschool?

In order to maximize your earnings, we recommend 4 to 8 PM on weekdays & all day weekends as peak student hours.


How Much Time Is Required To Become A Questschool?

The minimum time commitment is 2 hours a week, one hour a day for 2 days a week as a Quest is 2 classes a week for 6 weeks.


Do I Need Teaching Experience To Become A Questschool?

No experience or degree is required to become a Questschool. All you need is passion for education, empathy towards kids and growth mindset to learn new things on a daily basis with fluent communication.


What If I Have An Emergency & Cannot Take The Class?

Geekz values commitment & punctuality but we can understand your emergency situations. Kindly communicate before 24 hours & reschedule your class to another day by end of the Quest with parents' consent. Reschedule only during real emergencies as parents will move out to other Questschools if this happens often. If you're a No-Show (no or less than 24 hours communication & reschedule) for a class, you'll forgo your class earnings as penalty and if it happens second time, we'll have to regretfully remove you from the platform.


What Will Be My Agreement With Geekz?

You will be having terms & conditions to agree while applying to Become a Questschool such as upholding promises, responsibilities, quality of education, not sharing, subleasing, replicating or reusing Geekz intellectual property, code of conduct, customer relationship, performance, studio maintenance and a mutual consent of termination with 6-week notice. You agree to Geekz terms & conditions on applying to Become a Questschool.
